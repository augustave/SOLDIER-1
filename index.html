03. Initiative
Charter

Klassifikation: Concept Framework

The Future Soldier 2030 Initiative is a conceptual capability framework — not established doctrine — developed to identify what soldiers will require in future combat environments and to guide investment, stimulate technical dialogue, and evaluate trade-offs across the warfighter system.

Umfang (Scope) Verzeichnungsbogen (Details) Status
Lead Org NSRDEC leads development as RDECOM's Soldier System Integration Domain head. Additional input from contributing RDECs, PEO-Soldier, and designated Knowledge Centers. [ LEAD ]
Intent Not established doctrine. Guides investment priorities, stimulates dialogue, and evaluates technological trade-offs for the future warfighter. [ ADVISORY ]
Outcome Seven cross-cutting functional domains, 27 entity nodes, 31 documented relationships forming the integrated soldier-as-a-system architecture. [ VERIFIED ]
